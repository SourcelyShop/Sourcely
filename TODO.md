
IDEAS:

For a premium account on a digital asset marketplace like Resourced, you want features that offer status, visibility, and personal branding.

Here are some high-impact customization ideas:

1. Visual Dominance (Status)
Animated Profile Picture & Banner: Allow them to upload GIFs or WebM files for their avatar and cover image. Motion catches the eye immediately.
Glowing Name/Frame: A subtle neon glow effect around their username or avatar in comments and listings.
Custom Color Themes: Let them pick a "Brand Color" (e.g., Cyberpunk Pink, Gold, Electric Blue) that overrides the default accent color on their profile page and asset pages.
2. Listing Superpowers (Visibility)
"Verified" or "Pro" Badge: A distinct checkmark or icon that signals trust.
Video Covers: Allow them to use a short video loop as the main thumbnail for their assets in the search grid (instead of a static image).
Rich Text Descriptions: Unlock advanced formatting (embedded YouTube videos, tables, colored text) for their asset descriptions.
3. Vanity & Branding
Custom URL: resourced.io/u/DesignGod instead of resourced.io/u/12345-xyz.
Pinned Assets: Let them pin their best-selling or favorite 3 assets to the very top of their profile.
My Recommendation: Start with Custom Color Themes and Animated Avatars. They are visually striking and make the user feel like they "own" their space on your platform.
